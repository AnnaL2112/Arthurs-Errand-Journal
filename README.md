# Arthur's Errand Journal

A free **Red Dead Redemption 2** field-journal tracker for completion runs, challenges, collectibles, mysteries, hunting, treasure, chapter errands, camp chores and personal notes.

## Open the app

👉 **[Open Arthur's Errand Journal](https://annal2112.github.io/Arthurs-Errand-Journal/)**

No login required. Your playthroughs save in your own browser. Use export/import inside the app to back up your saves or move them to another device.

## What it tracks

Multiple playthroughs, including Vanilla and Modded runs
100% completion progress
Challenges with nested tasks, timers and quantity trackers
Satchel upgrades, camp upgrades and pelt/material planning
Collectibles, legendary animals, legendary fish and compendium progress
Chapter errands, gang requests, missables and honor-sensitive reminders
Mysteries, strange encounters, treasure maps, hidden riches and curated cash stashes
Optional errands like hats, masks, unique loot, keepsakes and personal player setups
Trail Journal notes with custom tags, search, filtering and pinned entries

## How saves work

Arthur's Errand Journal uses browser local storage. That means your playthrough data stays on your own device/browser and is not uploaded anywhere.

Because saves are local, you should export your playthrough JSON periodically as a backup, especially before clearing browser data or switching devices.

## Downloading

You can use the live app link above, or download this repository as a ZIP from GitHub and open `index.html` in your browser.
